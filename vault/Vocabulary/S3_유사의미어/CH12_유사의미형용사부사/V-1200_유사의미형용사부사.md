# 유사의미 형용사·부사

## 단어 목록

| 단어 | 의미 | 혼동 단어 | 차이점/구별법 | 예문 |
|------|------|-----------|---------------|------|
| considerable | 상당한 (양·정도) | considerate | considerable(양·정도가 상당한); considerate(배려심 있는) — 철자 혼동 주의 | The renovation project required considerable time and financial resources. |
| considerate | 배려심 있는 | considerable | 사람의 성품을 묘사; be considerate of others | The manager was considerate of her team's work-life balance. |
| economic | 경제의, 경제적인 | economical | economic(경제에 관한); economical(비용 절약이 되는) — 수식 명사로 구별 | The government announced new economic measures to boost growth. |
| economical | 경제적인, 절약이 되는 | economic | economical = cost-effective; 연료·자원을 적게 쓰는 | This fuel-efficient vehicle is both environmentally friendly and economical. |
| successful | 성공한 | successive | successful(성공적인); successive(연속적인) — 철자 혼동 주의 | The company launched a successful marketing campaign last quarter. |
| successive | 연속적인 | successful | successive = consecutive; 특히 'for the third successive year(3년 연속)' 패턴 | Sales increased for the fifth successive quarter, breaking a company record. |
| appropriate | 적절한 | approximate | appropriate(상황에 맞는); approximate(대략적인) | Please use appropriate language in all business communications. |
| approximate | 대략적인, 어림잡은 | appropriate | approximate는 정확하지 않은 수치·시간을 나타냄; approximately adv. | The approximate cost of the renovation is $200,000. |
| responsible | 책임이 있는 | responsive | responsible(책임 있는 — be responsible for); responsive(반응하는 — be responsive to) | The project manager is responsible for ensuring all deadlines are met. |
| responsive | 반응하는, 대응하는 | responsible | responsive to feedback(피드백에 민감하게 반응하는); 고객·시장 반응성 | Our customer service team is highly responsive to client inquiries. |
| confidential | 기밀의 | confident | confidential(기밀의 — 정보 보안); confident(자신감 있는 — 사람의 태도) | All client information must be kept strictly confidential. |
| confident | 자신감 있는 | confidential | confident about/in(~에 자신감 있는); confidence n. 자신감 | The sales team is confident about achieving this year's targets. |
| shortly | 곧, 금방 | briefly | shortly(시간상 곧); briefly(짧은 시간 동안); shortly after/before | The chairman will make an announcement shortly after the meeting begins. |
| briefly | 잠시, 간략히 | shortly | briefly(짧게·간략하게); speak briefly(간략히 말하다) | The CEO briefly addressed the press before entering the building. |
| currently | 현재 | recently | currently(지금 이 순간 진행 중); recently(최근에 완료된 것) — 시제와 연결 | The company is currently reviewing applications for the open position. |
| recently | 최근에 | currently | recently는 과거에 완료된 일을 나타낼 때; 현재완료·과거 시제와 함께 | The firm recently opened a new branch in Singapore. |
| significantly | 상당히 | marginally | significantly(크게, 상당히); marginally(미미하게, 근소하게) — 정도의 차이 | Profits increased significantly after the launch of the new product line. |
| marginally | 아주 조금, 근소하게 | significantly | marginally(미미한 차이); marginally better/lower 패턴 | The new model is only marginally more expensive than the previous version. |
| primarily | 주로, 주된 것으로 | partly | primarily(주로, 대부분); partly(부분적으로) — 비율의 차이 | The conference is designed primarily for senior management. |
| formerly | 이전에, 예전에 | formally | formerly(과거에); formally(공식적으로) — 철자 혼동 주의 | The building formerly served as a warehouse before its renovation. |
| formally | 공식적으로 | formerly | formally announced(공식 발표됨); formal adj. 공식적인 | The merger was formally announced at a press conference yesterday. |

## 출제 포인트

- **철자 혼동 쌍 암기 필수**: considerable/considerate, economic/economical, successful/successive, appropriate/approximate, responsible/responsive, confidential/confident, formerly/formally
- **economic vs. economical**: economic policy(경제 정책), economical car(연비 좋은 차) — 수식 명사의 성격으로 구별
- **currently vs. recently**: currently(현재진행형 시제와 호응), recently(현재완료·과거 시제와 호응)
- **significantly vs. marginally**: 정도 부사이므로 문맥 속 변화의 크기로 판단
- **shortly vs. briefly**: shortly(곧 — 시간 임박), briefly(잠깐 — 지속 시간 짧음)
